Caesar salad

Description

Caesar salad is a healthy option to provide your body with vitamins and minerals this summer, such as calcium, iron or vitamins A, C and K. Lettuce and bread can provide fiber and carbohydrates, while chicken is a good source of lean protein with high biological value.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices Sandwich Bread Cut into Cubes
  • 1 Cup Chicken Breast Fillets
  • 1 Sachet Maggi® Criollita Broth
  • 2 Units Romaine Lettuce, well washed
  • 1/2 Cup Maggi® Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tablespoons Maggi® Mustard
  • 1/4 Cup Vinegar

Preparation

  1. For the bread cubes: Place the bread cubes in a baking dish with some oil, salt and black pepper to taste. Let them brown in a preheated oven at 180º C for 15 minutes. Once ready, remove and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken: Season the chicken breast strips with MAGGI® Criollita Broth and brown on a griddle or frying pan. Once ready, set aside.
  3. Prepare the sauce: To prepare the sauce, blend MAGGI® Mayonnaise, MAGGI® Mustard, vinegar, capers, Parmesan cheese and anchovies in a blender. Blend for 5 minutes and set aside.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Cut the lettuce and place it on a plate, add croutons (golden bread), chicken strips and the dressing in a separate sauce boat and serve.
